Safe Sitting and Nanny Solutions

Our Mission

Thank you for entrusting your children's safety with
Baby Corp referred Professional Sitters.
This is our mission, to help families nationwide
understand the importance of a proper screening in hopes to stop
unnecessary harm that could have been prevented had a
thorough background screening been performed.

We personally know why it is so important to perform
a thorough background
investigation on every sitter who cares for your children.

Through these experiences, we work to build the safest
sitting community for families across the nation.

How it Works

Whether you need Baby Corp for a one-time occasion
or a full-time need, each family must be approved through
the member verification process. The online application is easy
to fill out and you can even start requesting sitters the same day!

We do all the work for you!

As soon as your membership is approved, you will
have access to your personal online account where
you can request sitters, view the photos and
profiles of your area's current babysitting team, update your family's profile and
set your sitter preferences. You can also request activation in another Baby Corp location for use on travels.

On Demand

And the best part, all of this is delivered
on-demand.
Request a fully vetted, experienced sitter from the
convenience of your phone and sit back while Baby Corp does the rest.
We have actively working professional sitters
ready to work at a moment's notice.
